Save the Reefs!

Todays plastic filled reefs have many problems for many species, and we're researching how proper ecotourism can fix that.

How does your behavior affect the ocean?

Our behavior, from stealing little pieces of coral, to throwing away your plastic bottles in the water, to smashing spirit bottles in the sand, all adversely affect the environment. That little piece of of coral you took because it looked colorful and nice? Outside of the water it greys and dies. the plastic bottle you threw in the water? it will kill an unsuspecting fish, looking for a meal. That spirit bottle you broke on the beach? it'll impale a baby turtle in the stomach and kill it. so join us in saving these animals, with simple good behavior.
